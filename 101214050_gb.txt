George Brown College is located in Toronto where they have many campuses located around the city- There is the St. James Campus, Casa Loma Campus & the Waterfront Campus.
They officially opened in 1968!
Given the variety of campuses offered George Brown College offers many programs for their students so they are prepared for the job market!
They offer programs in construction, early child hood education, dentistry & fashion.
They offer a wide range of curriculum so that students can gain hands on experience & skills for their future!
With a population of over 30,000 they are continously to grow!
